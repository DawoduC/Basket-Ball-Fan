About me and my love for Basketball & American Football

Hi my name is Charles and the first thing I ever loved in life is basketball and my LA Lakers team!! [Outside family and food ofcourse ;)]

I've never had a chance to play competitively as I was not aware of any teams until I got into secondary school. Once I got into secondary and I was aware there was a team I was eager to try-out and play for the school team.
I WAS CUT!! :( I was not successful in making the team. Gutted I was aspired by a hero of mine MICHAEL JORDAN! Who also was cut in his 1st year of high school and is now considered the greatest of all time. So I continued playing and training whenever I had any free time, and then in year 8 despite still not being in the team for half the season and opportunity came about when a player was injuired and the team needed a replacement. I was the first person they sought. Coming off the bench on my first game I balled out, my defense was impeccable, I had so many steals that night and enabled to score double digits in points, and we won the game. Sidenote I made a kid cry due to the amount of steals I had on him lol

Despite playing well that game, I was very inconsistent, and I was starting to get distracted with activities that I had took upon since yr7 which was turning me into somewhat of a delinquent i.e. gang, fighting, bunking lessons,
chasing girls. Then in year 9 I made the decision to take basketball more seriously. So I trained everyday before school and after, also joined a local team outside school to assist my development. My behaviour changed and I was focusing more in school and didn't have time for the extra troubling activities. That year, my school finally made it into the finals, and despite the loss I was motivated to get back there.

That moment didn't come around until my last year of secondary school (yr11). The year I was named team captain for the first time. Being team was difficult because I was motivated but getting the rest motivated to come in early and train and play hard was close to impossible that year. But I got it done and my play lead the example as I lead the team in points and rebounds, on our way to the finals. *Remember the kid I made cry* Well I played him and his team in the finals lool!

It was a close game throughout, leading the team in scoring I made final shot and we was up by three with roughly 5 seconds left. Now this kid *who I made cry* was a 3pt specialist and he had the ball, my teammate was guarding him. With the shot-clock winding down and I came over to help and the kid took his 3pt shot just I managed to contest, and he made it!!

BUT IT WAS TOO LATE! I look over to the ref and he waived it off. The buzzer had went off before he released the ball. MY TEAM WON!! I was the MVP and my team won it's first ever championship.

It is momements like this I will never forget. Basketball was everything to me at this point. It saved my life and made me a success. It taught me so many lessons.

And no despite losing, the kid didn't cry unfortunately

This website is a dedication to the sports that saved my life and taught me so much
